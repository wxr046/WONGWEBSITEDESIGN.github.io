Simple Spange cake

Ingredients
  • 3 eggs
  • 100g castor suger
  • 85g self-raising flour

Notes on Ingredients
Castor Suger
Finely groulated white suger
Self raising Flour
A pre-mixed combination of flour and leavering agents (usually salt and baking pouder)

Method
  1. Preheat the oven to 190oC.
  2. Grease a 20cm round cake pan.
  3. In a medium bowl, whip together the eggs and castor suger until fluffy.
  4. Fold in flour
  5. Pour mixture into the prepared pan.
  6. Bake 20 minute in the preheated oven until the top of the cake spring back when light pressed.
  7. Cool in the pan over a wire rack.
